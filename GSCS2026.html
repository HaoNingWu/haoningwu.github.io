2026 Georgia Scientific Computing Symposium

Saturday, February 21, 2026
Physics Building 202, University of Georgia


The Georgia Scientific Computing Symposium is a forum for professors, postdocs, graduate students and other researchers in Georgia to meet in an informal setting, to exchange ideas, and to highlight local scientific computing research. Held every year since 2009, the symposium welcomes the entire research community. This year’s event will take place on Saturday, February 21, 2026, at the University of Georgia. The day-long program will feature invited talks, poster sessions with a “poster blitz,” and ample opportunities for networking.

Registration Please fill out the registration form. While registration is free, we kindly request that participants register by Friday, February 13, to facilitate event planning and logistics. For registration inquiries, please contact Hao-Ning Wu at hnwu@uga.edu.

Plenary Speakers

  • Ming-Jun Lai, University of Georgia

  • Helen Xu, Georgia Institute of Technology, CSE
  • Chi-Kuang Yeh, Georgia State University
  • Haomin Zhou, Georgia Institute of Technology, Math

Poster Sessions

Poster presentations are open to all; we especially welcome graduate students and postdocs to advertise their work. To participate, please include your poster title in the registration form. You’ll also be invited to our Poster Blitz for a one-minute, one-slide preview. Please upload your slide once you have completed the registration form.
