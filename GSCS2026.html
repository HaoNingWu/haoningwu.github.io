2026 Georgia Scientific Computing Symposium

Saturday, February 21, 2026
Miller Learning Center, University of Georgia

The Georgia Scientific Computing Symposium is a forum for professors, postdocs, graduate students and other researchers in Georgia to meet in an informal setting, to exchange ideas, and to highlight local scientific computing research. Held every year since 2009, the symposium welcomes the entire research community. This year’s event will take place on Saturday, February 21, 2026, at the University of Georgia. The day-long program will feature invited talks, poster sessions with a “poster blitz,” and ample opportunities for networking.
